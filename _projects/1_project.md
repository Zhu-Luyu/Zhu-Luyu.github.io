Exploring the adaptation of Genie Envisioner (a unified world foundation platform for manipulation) to Embodied Navigation (VLN) tasks, aiming to solve data scarcity in navigation through generative world models.

### Methodology
Investigating the transfer of Action-Conditioned Video Diffusion Models (based on LTX-Video/DiT architecture) from manipulation domains to VLN scenes (e.g., Matterport3D/Habitat).

### Current Focus
Designing a pipeline to convert VLN datasets (R2R) into video-action pairs to fine-tune the Video DiT backbone, enabling the agent to predict navigation trajectories.
