Developed a novel AI-generated content attribution method using pretrained diffusion models as denoising autoencoders for feature extraction.

### Results
- Achieved 15% and 3% higher accuracy than previous state-of-the-art methods on DiffusionForensics and Artifact datasets respectively
- Demonstrated framework-agnostic and self-supervised feature extraction capabilities
- Paper accepted at IEEE ICASSP 2025 (CCF-B Conference)
